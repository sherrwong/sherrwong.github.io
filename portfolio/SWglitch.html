Glitch art

With these piece I used 2 different methods to create these glitch effects "text edit/ databending and audacity".

Piece #1

-All Eyes On Gary-

In this image you'll see is Gary the snail, and by using audacity I wanted to enhance and show people that Gary always has his eyes on everyone. To me in the show it almost felt as if Gary was always watching out for Spongebob and his friends which the hidden intention I want to show people.

Piece #2

-Patrick Inner Beauty -

As for this image you'll see Patrick the Star, and by using audacity I wanted to show Patrick's inner beauty and what he really has besides what he shows

Piece #3

-Just Plankton-

As for this image you have the infamous Plankton, and by using "textedit or databending" I wanted to show the other side of Plankton where a small part of him just wants to be happy but like at the bottom of this image he is overpowered by his hatred " the evil red laugh". But in the middle right image u can see a little excited smile.
